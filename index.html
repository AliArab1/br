Simple examples

Printer stuck? Clear the print queue, restart the spooler, re-test.
Wi-Fi broken? Reset the adapter and network settings, reconnect.
Mic/webcam dead? Reset permissions, rebind the device, quick test.
VPN/DNS failing? Flush DNS, repair split-tunnel, reachability check.
Fake login page? Close the tab, lock the session, revoke tokens.
